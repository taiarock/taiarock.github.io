Blog do Lula

User Interface + Information Architecture + ID

Prototype of a Interface design for the former Brazil President Lula da Silva’s blog, in development while working for MPI Digital. The blog’s objective was to be a direct tools with its former voters, thus a simple and clean interface was chosen.

Logo Design + Storyteller

Handwritten Typography to incur in a homemade feeling. And in the background pictures telling part of the former president’s history

Usability

Few options in the menus, well defined markings on the active page, and breadcrumbs indicating the way back :)

easy search + contrast

Highlight of the searched word in the title as well as in the post’s content. The same is made with a combination of color and font-weight

comments in the same place

Since the comment would be moderated or even blocked, I opted for placing them in a modal and not below of each post as usually is made
